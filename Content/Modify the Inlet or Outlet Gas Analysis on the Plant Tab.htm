Modify the Inlet or Outlet Gas Analysis on the Plant Tab

To modify the Inlet or Outlet gas analysis on Predictions | Data | Plant

  1. Do one of the following:
    2. To modify Select this Input Mode
    Inlet Gas Analysis Raw or Inlet ratios
    Outlet Gas Analysis Sales ratios
    When you select an input mode, the corresponding gas analysis is highlighted in blue.
  2. Edit a value and press Enter.

If the total does not equal 100, the Total value is displayed in red.

Three Theoretical Yields are displayed below the analyses. See Theoretical Yield.

Also see Add a Gas Analysis.
