Create Groups on the Map

You can also point to a location on the Map tab and create a group. The Latitude and Longitude of your mouse pointer position are displayed at the bottom left of the map.

To create a group on the map

  1. Click the Map tab.
  2. Hold the Ctrl button and click the wells you want to use in the group to select them.
  3. In the entity Explorer, right-click and click Create as Group.
  4. Type a name for the group
  5. Under Phase, select Oil or Gas.
  6. Under Calculate Reserves Categories, select the reserves categories you want to calculate.
  7. Edit other group information, as required, and click OK.
You must select Wells and Groups or Groups from the Entity Type list in the Standard toolbar in order to see your group once you have created it.
