Enter Alberta Capital Cost Index (ACCI)

Wells in Alberta that operate under the Modernized Royalty Framework will have the ACCI applied when royalties are calculated.

The C* value calculated on the Interests and Royalties tab is not factored by ACCI. The ACCCI is applied when economics are run.

To enter the ACCI

  1. On the Tools menu, point to Global Project Data and select Price Decks.
  2. In the active price deck (and any scenario price decks), select Tax Rates and browse to Alberta Capital Cost Index.
  3. Enter the index value (%) on the required year.

The ACCI tracks year-over-year inflationary or deflationary changes in the industry. The ACCI will initially be set to 100% in 2017, and allowed to “float” on an annual basis as a function of changes in industry costs. The value will be periodically modified by the Alberta government.
