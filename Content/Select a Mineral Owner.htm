Select a Mineral Owner

To select a mineral owner

  1. Go to Economics | Interests & Royalties.
  2. Under Mineral Owner, select Crown, Indian Lands or one of the following Freehold types:
    3. Owner Type Description
    Freehold (Proportionate Share) The WI Owner and the Freehold Owner will each pay their share of the Mineral Tax owning. For example, If there is a 15% Freehold Owner then the WI owner will pay 85% of the owing Mineral Tax
    Freehold (Lessor pays mineral tax) The Lessor (Freehold owner) pays all owing Mineral Tax. For example, If there is a 15% Freehold Owner then the WI owner will pay 0% of the owing Mineral Tax.
    Freehold (Lessee pays mineral tax) The Lessee (WI owner) pays all owing Mineral Tax. For example, If there is a 15% Freehold Owner then the WI owner will pay 100% of the owing Mineral Tax

See Enter Freehold Royalties (Payable or Receivable).
