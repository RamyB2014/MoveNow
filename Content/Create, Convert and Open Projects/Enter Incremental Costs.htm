Enter Incremental Costs

When you use the Incremental forecasting mode (see Create an Incremental Forecast), you only need to add the incremental portion of your operating or capital costs. However, you must also create new Incremental costs by overriding the capital or operating costs tab, creating a new cost, and entering the incremental amount.

Entering Incremental Costs does not apply to the PDP reserves category.

To enter an incremental operating or capital cost

  1. Go to the Operating or Capital Costs tab and click Override.
  2. Add a new cost.
    The Base operating or capital cost is displayed with a light blue bar below the cost name. The new incremental cost is displayed with a dark blue bar.
  3. Enter the incremental portion of the cost in the date it is to be incurred.
  4. Also see Enter Operating Costs and Enter Captial Costs.
