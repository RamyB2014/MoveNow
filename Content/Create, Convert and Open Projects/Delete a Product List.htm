Delete a Product List

You can only delete a custom product list from your project if the product list is not being used by an entity.

To delete a custom product list from your project

  1. Ensure no entities are using the product list you want to delete.
    2. If required, use the filter to find any entities using the product list you want to delete.
  2. In the Tools menu, point to Global Project Data and click Products.
  3. Click the Product Lists tab.
  4. In the list on the left, click the product list you want to remove.
  5. Click Remove.
