Add a Transition to a Decline

If a well has a hyperbolic or harmonic decline, you can add an exponential transition to the end of the decline by clicking Add Transition on Predictions | Declines.

To add a transitions

  1. On Predictions | Declines, select a well with a harmonic or hyperbolic declines.
  2. Click the name (such as Gas or Oil) to display the detail view (decline parameters).
  3. To the right of the decline parameters, click Add Transition.
You can also press T on the keyboard while in Hotkey mode to add a transition.
