Common Termination Entity

A common termination entity (CTE) consists of at least one well and one facility (cost entity) in the same field. Creating a CTE enables you to determine the economic limit of the field by ignoring well-level economic limits and calculating the economics for all the wells and facilities as one entity.

You can add a CTE to another CTE or Ring Fence Entity by right clicking the CTE in the explorer and selecting Add to > CTE or Add to > Ring Fence Entity.

Creating a CTE involves the three basic steps below. See the following sections for more details.

  1. Filter to Wells or Organize the Wells by Field.
  2. Create a Cost Entity(facility) and assigning it costs.
  3. Create a CTE.
