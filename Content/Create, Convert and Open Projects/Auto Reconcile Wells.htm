Auto Reconcile Wells

You can auto-reconcile wells in the Change Management menu or on Review | Waterfall or Review | Change Records by clicking Reconcile.

The Balance button is for creating change records manually.

To auto-reconcile an entity or entities

  1. In the entity hierarchy, select the entity, entities (using CTRL + click or SHIFT + click), or folder you want to reconcile.
  2. Go to one of the locations below and click Reconcile:
    • Change Management menu
    • Review | Waterfall
    • Review | Change Records
  1. If required, select a Jurisdiction and click OK.

Change records are created and displayed on Review | Change Records. You can also view the results of the reconciliation on Review | Waterfall.
