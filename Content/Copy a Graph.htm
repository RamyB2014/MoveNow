Copy a Graph

Any graphs that you create in can be exported from the program as a graph or as a table. When you copy a graph as an image, the image of the graph remains on your clipboard and can be pasted into other programs. When you copy a graph as a table, the information from the table is added to the clipboard as a table that can be pasted into spreadsheet applications.

To create a copy of a graph that you can edit, see Create a Graph Copy .

To copy a graph

  1. Right-click the graph you want to copy.
  2. Do one of the following:
    3. If Then
    You selected Copy as Image, Open a word processor or graphics program and press Ctrl+V on the keyboard.
    You selected Copy as Table, Open a spreadsheet program, such as Excel, and press Ctrl+V on the keyboard.
With stacked graphs, the stacked values are copied. For gas wells, gas is at the bottom, then oil, and then water. For oil wells, oil is on the bottom, then gas, then water.
