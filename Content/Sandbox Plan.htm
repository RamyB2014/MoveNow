Sandbox Plan

The Sandbox plan is a built-in plan you can use for “what if” scenarios. The Sandbox plan is not included in reserves and does not affect other plans (unless they are children of the Sandbox).

The Sandbox plan is always a top-level plan. You cannot delete it or make it secure, but you can add new plans to it as children.

You can copy entities or folders to the Sandbox from the Entity menu (Copy to Sandbox).

When you copy to the Sandbox, any corresponding entity or folder level data already in the Sandbox is overwritten.
