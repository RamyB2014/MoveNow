Pin Entities to the List on Comparison | Economic

When you edit the list on Comparison | Economic, it is automatically updated and all rows that are not pinned to the list are removed. To retain a row when you edit the selections of other rows, pin the row to the list.

To pin a row to the list, do one of the following:

  1. Click the pin on the right of the row.
  2. Right-click the row and select Pin selection.
