Filter to Selected Entities on the Map

To select and filter to entities on the map

  1. On the Map tab, click  to enter selection mode.
  2. Do one of the following on the map:
    • Right-click an entity
    • Hold the left mouse button and drag the square around several entities
  1. Right-click the selection and select Filter to Selection to filter the Entity Hierarchy to the selected entities.

To clear the filter, click  in the main toolbar.
