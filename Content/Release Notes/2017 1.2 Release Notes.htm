2017 1.2 Release Notes

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed an issue with O+W forecasts in combination with 1+WOR where the forecast duration could change upon well copying or promotion. This would lead to unbalanced results in the Pending or Accepted copies after promoting from a balanced Working copy.
  • Fixed an issue with the O+W calculation where it would incorrectly determine the point of a max-rate transition. This would manifest in different ways:
  • Segment duration shown on the Declines tab not matching the graph
  • Final rate (Qf) on O+W segments not always being honoured
  • Forecasts terminating at O+W end date instead of oil end date
  • Production gaps at segment transitions
