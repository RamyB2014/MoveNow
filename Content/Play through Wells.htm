Play through Wells

Using the Navigation toolbar, you can play through the wells in your project to perform a quick evaluation of each well. As each well is displayed, you can pause and make adjustments or flag the well for a closer look later. You can move the Navigation toolbar closer to the area of the application you are working in for easy access. To adjust the play speed, see Adjust Play Speed.
