Flag and Filter Entities

You can flag entities you have currently selected in the entity hierarchy and then filter to them later. You can view the entities you have flagged on Wells and General Economics | Well Info and Custom Fields in the Data View.

To flag single entities, do one of the following

  • Highlight a well in the Entity Explorer and click Flag Current Hierarchy Selection  in the toolbar.
  • On Wells and General Economics | Well Info and Custom Fields (in Data View), click the checkbox in the Flagged column
  • In any of the Data View tabs, select an entity, right-click and select Select Entities in the Entity Explorer. Then click  in the toolbar.

Once you have set flags, click  and select Turn Flag Filter On to filter to the flagged wells.

To toggle the flag filter on and off click  and click Turn Flag Filter On or Turn Flag Filter Off.
