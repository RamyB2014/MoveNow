View Best Fit Forecasts on Predictions | Workspace

The Best Fit function enables you to best fit wells and view the results on Predictions | Workspace where you can compare the results of several forecasts with different fit options. From Predictions | Workspace you can then determine which forecast you want to apply to the well.

To apply a Best Fit and view the results in the Decline Workspace

  1. Follow the Best Fit procedure and select Create in Workspace under Fit Target.
  2. Repeat step 1 with different Best Fit options, if required, and assign the forecast(s) a new name.
  3. Go to Predictions | Workspace to view the best fit declines you created.
  4. Under Display, select the forecast(s) you want to display and compare the forecasts you created.
  5. Apply a forecast to a reserves category and plan.
