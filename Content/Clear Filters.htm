Clear Filters

When you have applied more than one type of filter, you can clear one filter without clearing the others. You can also clear all the filters at once.

Option 1: Clear One Filter

To clear one filter only

  1. On the Standard toolbar click .
  2. Do one of the following:
    3. To Do this
    Clear a filter query, 1. Click Filter Well List. 2. In the Filter dialog box, click Clear Active Filter.
    To clear a flag filter, Click Turn Flag Filter Off.
    To clear a selection filter, Click Clear Selection Filter.

Option 2: Clear All Filters

To clear all filters, click  on the Standard toolbar.

Clearing a filter does not remove flags from entities.
