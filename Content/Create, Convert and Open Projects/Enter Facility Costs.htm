Enter Facility Costs

If it is necessary to have a different working interest applied to facility capital or operating costs, facility capital costs, abandonment costs, and salvage costs can be entered on Economics | Prices & Costs | Capital Costs. Facility operating costs are entered on Economics |Prices & Costs | Op Costs/Other Revenue. These costs will be applied according to the Facility Interest on Economics | Interest and Royalties. See Enter Capital Costs and Enter Operating Costs.
