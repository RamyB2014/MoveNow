Filter to Wells or Organize the Wells by Field

Before creating a Common Termination Entity (CTE), you can do one of the following:

  • Organize your wells by Field (see Create or Edit an Entity Hierarchy) and then create a cost entity based on the wells in the Field folder.
  • Create a cost entity and then filter to the cost entity and the wells you want to include in the CTE (and then create the CTE from those entities).
