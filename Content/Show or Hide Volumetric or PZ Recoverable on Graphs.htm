Show or Hide Volumetric or P/Z Recoverable on Graphs

All default graphs are read-only, but you can make copies of the graph and edit its options.

To show or hide Volumetric or P/Z recoverable on a graph

  1. On Predictions | Declines or Predictions | Volumetrics, right-click any graph and click Select/Manage Graphs.
  2. In the Select/Manage Graphs dialog box, click the report you want to make a copy of and click Edit.
    The Graph Version Manager is displayed.
  3. Select the graph version you want to copy and click Edit. 
    “This graph is read-only. Would you like to edit a copy” is displayed.
  4. Click Yes.
    The Graph Designer is displayed.
  5. In the Graph Designer, click .
  6. For Volumetrics recoverable or P/Z recoverable, select Hide or Show.
  7. Click Save.
  8. Enter a name for new graph.
  9. Close the Graph Editor and Graph Version Manager and select the new graph in the Select\Manage Graphs dialog box.
  10. Click OK.
