Enter a Min/Max Payable on Crown Equivalent

You can enter a minimum and maximum Crown Equivalent percentage for oil or gas leases on Freehold or Indian Lands.

To enter a minimum and maximum

  1. On Economics | Interests & Royalties, select the interest to which you want to apply the minimum and maximum.
  2. Under Entity Properties, select a Royalty Calculation.
    Royalty parameters are displayed on the right.
  3. For Owner Type, select Indian Lands or any Freehold type.
  4. For Calculation Type, select Crown Equivalent.
  5. Select the Production Category and Incentive Type, if applicable.>
  6. Click  next to Royalty (%).
  7. For Oil and/or Gas, enter a minimum and maximum value and click OK.
