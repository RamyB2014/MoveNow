Compare Results Overview

On the Comparison tab, you can compare economic or technical data between entities or within different plans or reserves categories in one entity.

On Comparison | Economic, you can compare production, cash flow, and costs in different archives, reserves plans, calculated plans, reserves categories, and scenarios.

For economic comparisons, keep in mind that:

  • All results are based on economic calculations and require economics to be run.
  • Only results after the Economic Calculation Start Date are displayed.
  • Results are reported on a monthly basis until the Last Year for Monthly Output. Then results are displayed annually.
  • Production is based on the entity’s Economic Limit (if that option is enabled).
  • On Comparison | Technical, you can compare declines between Reserves Categories and Plans within one entity.
