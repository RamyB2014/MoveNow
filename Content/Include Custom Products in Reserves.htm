Include Custom Products in Reserves

Default products (such as oil, gas, and condensate) are automatically included in reserves management. However, if you want to include additional products (such as injection gas or injection oil) or custom products you have created, you must add them to a jurisdiction.

To add a custom product to a jurisdiction
  1. In the Tools menu, point to Global Project Data and click Jurisdictions.
  2. In the jurisdictions list on the left, select the jurisdiction.
  3. In the Additional Products in Reserve Reports list (under Posting/Balancing), select the additional products and click OK.
