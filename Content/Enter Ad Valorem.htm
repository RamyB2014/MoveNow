Enter Ad Valorem

If Ad Valorem has been set up in a fiscal regime as an entity-level input, it is displayed on Economics | Interest & Royalties if the Before Tax Calculation is selected for that regime. Ad Valorem can only be added to entities designated as US or International.

To enter an Ad Valorem percentage

  1. Go to Economics | Interest & Royalties. The Ad Valorem table is displayed beneath the Interest Properties.
  2. Enter the Ad Valorem percentages in the applicable dates.
You can copy Ad Valorem from one entity to others using the Input Copier.
