Perform a Gas Pool Material Balance

The following procedures are a suggested workflow for performing a gas pool material balance. Not all steps are always necessary and the order in which they are performed can be altered in some cases.

To perform a gas pool material balance

  1. Create a group of the wells to include in the analysis.
  2. Deselect wells that don’t belong to the pool.
  3. Deselect unwanted gas analyses.
  4. Enter the Corrected Pool Datum and Recovery Factor (or abandonment pressure).
  5. Deselect unwanted BHP tests.
  6. Edit the declines of the child wells.
  7. Clear the manual decline and generate a group forecast.
  8. Adjust the group final rate.
  9. Link the decline to the P/Z analysis.
