Exclude Entities from Jurisdictions

When you create a jurisdiction, it includes all entities in the project by default.

If a well is not “In Reserves”, it is not included in any jurisdiction calculations or reports even if it has not been removed from a jurisdiction. See Designate Entities as In Reserves or Out Of Reserves

To remove an entity from a jurisdiction

  1. Go to Wells and General Economics | Well Info and Custom Fields.
  2. Scroll to the In Jurisdiction <jurisdiction name> column.
  3. Remove the checkmark from the entity you want to exclude.

  4. Click Save.
