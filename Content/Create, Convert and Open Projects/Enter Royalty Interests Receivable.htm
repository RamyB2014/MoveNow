Enter Royalty Interests (Receivable)

Enter Royalty interests (Receivable) on Economics | Interests & Royalties. The Interest types are explained below.

Royalty Interests Description
Freehold Interest (Receivable) This is the percentage of the freehold royalty proceeds, not the freehold royalty receivable. The allowances for transportation of oil and gas cost allowances for gas are received by the Freehold owner based upon the applied percentage. The working interest must be deleted in order to enter values for freehold interest.
Gross Overriding Royalty (= US Lease Royalty) Royalty receivable based on the gross proceeds from the production with no deduction for operating or capital costs.
Net Overriding Royalty (= US Overriding Royalty) Royalty receivable based on the net proceeds from the production (after royalty).
Sliding Scale Royalty The amount receivable based upon a formula which is a function of production with restrictions applied.
Net Profits Interest The amount to be received is a specified portion (%) of net profit from the property.
If the Royalty Interest is not displayed, click the Interest Properties list and select it.
