Best Fit or Refit Wells

When you best fit or refit a well, determines how much history to use in calculating the decline. To best fit or refit a well from a point in history or from a selection of history, see Best Fit or Refit a Decline from a Point or Best Fit or Refit a Decline from a Selection.

When you best fit a well, determines whether the decline should be hyperbolic, harmonic or exponential. When you refit a well, maintains the well’s current decline type.

To best fit or refit all the wells in your project, select the highest level folder in the Entity Explorer and follow the procedure below. If you only want to best fit some wells, select the wells individually or select the folder level that contains them or filter to them first.

Declines are created in reserves categories in accordance with the decline parameters specified for each category in your User Options: Product Specific. For example, selecting All Developed Producing in the Best Fit Options (below) will only create a decline in those reserves categories if you have entered decline parameters for them in the User Options. You can access the User Options by clicking the button in the bottom left corner of the Best Fit/Refit dialog box or by selecting Options from the Tools menu.

To best fit or refit wells

  1. In the entity explorer, select the entity or the folder level that contains the wells you want to best fit or refit.
  2. Do one of the following:
    • On the Predictions menu, click Best Fit or Refit
    • Click  on Predictions | Declines and click Best Fit or Refit
  1. In the Best Fit/Refit dialog box, select the appropriate options, below.
