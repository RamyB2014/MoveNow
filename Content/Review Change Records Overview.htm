Review | Change Records Overview

You can use the Review | Change Records tab to balance one entity (create a change record), view/edit change record data, or delete change records. See Balance Entities, Create or Edit Change Record Categories or Summary Types, and Delete Change Records.

In the change record display window on the left of Review | Change Records, change records are organized by change record category (if Group Records by Category is selected). The icons next to the change records indicate the reserves plan in which the change record resides (see the table, below). Note that the icon does not necessarily indicate the plan in which the record was created. When you copy data from one plan to another, the change records are also copied to the destination plan (a copy does not remain in the original plan).

When you are working in the Working plan, change records for all plans are visible in the display window on Review | Change Records. When you are working in Pending, only change records in Pending and Accepted are visible. When you are working in Accepted, only change records in Accepted are visible.

Reserves Plan Icon Change records visible on Review | Change Records
Working Working, Pending, Accepted
Pending Pending, Accepted
Accepted Accepted

In the grid at the bottom of Review | Change Records, a red dot () is displayed in the first column when an entity is unbalanced.

Use the Show Columns list to specify which data is displayed in the grid (Working Interest, Company Share, and Net).
