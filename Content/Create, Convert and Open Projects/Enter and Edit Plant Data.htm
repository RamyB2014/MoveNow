Enter and Edit Plant Data

Before entering plant data, you must have a Master Gas Analysis. If you need to add a Master Gas Analysis, see Add a Gas Analysis.

To enter or modify plant data such as Surface Loss, Process Loss, Fuel Loss, Energy Content, Ratios, or Efficiencies

  1. Go to Predictions | Data | Plant.
  2. Select an Input Mode (Raw, Inlet, or Sales).
  3. Modify the Inlet or Outlet Gas Analysis, if required.
  4. Select the data you want to display in the grid.
  5. Enter the Enter Surface Loss, Enter Process Loss or Fuel Loss
  6. Enter Volumes, Rates, Ratios, or Effiiciencies on the Plant Tab for each component.
