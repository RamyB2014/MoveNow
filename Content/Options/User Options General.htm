User Options: General

Reserves Category Display During Entity Selection

Keep current reserves category: The selected reserve category is displayed for each entity (well or folder) you select in the Entity Explorer. This enables you to keep viewing the same reserve category regardless of the well or folder you select.

Select first reserves category with data: The first reserve category with a decline or economic case is displayed when you select a well or folder in the Entity Explorer.

Play Delay (ms)

This option determines the play back speed (in milliseconds) when using the play button in the Controller Toolbar. See Play through Wells.

Use Sparse Reporting Logic

This option only applies to reports in the Batch Manager. It does not apply to reports on the Reports tab.

Enabled: Only reports for entities or folders where forecast and/or economic data exist will appear in the Print Preview.

Disabled: All reports will appear in the Print Preview, even if they do not have data to display.
