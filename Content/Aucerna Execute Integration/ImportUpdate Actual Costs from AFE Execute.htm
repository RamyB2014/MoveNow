Import/Update Actual Costs from AFENav

Before importing or updating actuals from AFENav, you must configure AFENav and ValNav integration.

When you import actuals, they are displayed on Economics | Prices & Costs | Capital Costs with a green background. Actuals are not editable in and are not used in economic calculations. Actuals provide information that can be used to make a more accurate capital cost forecast, which is used in economic calculations.

To display actuals, you must select Forecasts and Actuals from the View list in the top left.

If actuals are displayed with a red background, it means the actuals are not correctly mapped to a cost type. Contact your administrator to fix the error.

The blue line indicates the end of actuals. The difference between your forecasted cost and the actual cost is displayed below the End of Actuals line. This is the suggested forecast. If the suggested forecast is negative, you have spent more than you forecasted and should update the forecast to match the actuals.

Economics are run on forecasted capital costs by default (Run economics on forecast only option), but you can run economics on blended capital costs by selecting Run Economics on Blended Costs in the Project Options, under Economics. See Run Economics on Blended Capital Costs.
