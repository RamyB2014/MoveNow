Add (or Remove) a Company Logo to Reports

To add a company logo to reports

  1. From the Tools menu, select Options.
  2. In the Options dialog box, click the Project Options tab.
  3. Select Company Logo on the left.
  4. Do one of the following:
    5. To… Do this…
    Add a logo, Click Browse to select a logo (bmp, .png, .jpg). Go to step 5.
    Remove a logo, Click Clear. Go to step 6.
  5. From the Logo Position list, select a position.
  6. Click OK.
The logo is automatically resized, if required.
