Enter Manual Royalties

Manual Royalties are entered on Economics | Interests & Royalties.

You can enter manual royalties for a lease. For an American case (when the country of the case is United States in the Case Properties window), you can also add an Ad Valorem royalty.

To enter a manual royalty for a lease

  1. Select an interest in the Interests window.
  2. On the Interests tab, confirm your Entity Properties selections for the lease. The country you select determines the type of manual royalties you can enter.
  3. On the Royalty Properties tab, enter the Mineral Owner.
  4. Select Use Manual Royalties and click Edit.
  5. Enter the royalty percent beside the appropriate date. If you have selected an American case, you will have an additional column to enter Ad Valorem royalties.
  6. Click OK.
    Manual royalties appear as an Unadjusted Royalty in economics reports.
When a manual royalty is applied to an American case, this royalty will be added to the royalties of any fiscal regimes that are being applied to the case. To replace the fiscal regime with the manual royalty, deselect the fiscal regime.
