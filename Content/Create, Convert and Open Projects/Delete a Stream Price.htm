Delete a Stream Price

To delete a stream price

  1. In the Tools menu, select Global Project Data > Price Decks.
  2. In the Price Deck Editor, click Stream Prices (on the left).
  3. Select the stream price you want to delete.
  4. Click Remove, below the Price Streams window.
  5. In the Delete Stream Price dialog box, click Yes.
Deleting a stream price removes it from all price decks.
