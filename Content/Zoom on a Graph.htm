Zoom on a Graph

To zoom on a graph

  1. Hold the left mouse button down and drag a square around the area of the graph you want to enlarge.
  2. Release the left mouse button.
    The area you selected is enlarged.

To exit zoom, do one of the following:

  • Press Z on the keyboard.
  • Right-click the graph and select Zoom Out.
Printing the graph while zoomed prints the zoomed area.
