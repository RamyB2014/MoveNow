Escalate Prices

You can escalate a price by indicating an Escalation percentage (annual) in a price deck or on an entity.

Price escalation only applies to manually entered prices (overrides) and begins one year after the overridden value.

To escalate prices in a price deck, see Create a Stream Price.

To escalate operating costs, see Escalate Operating Costs.

To escalate prices on an entity

  1. On Economics | Prices & Costs | Prices, click Expand Details to display the Results columns and the Escalation % (annual) row.
  2. In a product’s Result column, enter the Escalation % (annual).
  3. In the year the escalation is to begin, enter the new price and press Enter.

The edited cell is displayed with a grey background to indicate a manual override of the stream price. The escalation of the price begins one year after the manual override.
