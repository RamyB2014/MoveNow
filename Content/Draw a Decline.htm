Draw a Decline

You can draw declines on the Predictions | Declines graphs using two methods. The results will differ depending on the method used.

Method 1: Drawing a Slope Before the Predict Line

transposes the slope to the forecast and gives the segment a Qi equal to the last production rate.

Method 2: Drawing a Slope through or after the Predict Line

uses the slope exactly as it is drawn and does not alter the forecast’s Qi.

To draw a decline

  1. On Predictions | Declines, right click the Rate/Cum graph.
  2. Point to the decline type and select Draw Gas Decline (or Draw Oil Decline, etc.).
    The Exponent option is displayed on the graph.
  3. Select Exponential or Harmonic.
  4. Hold the left mouse button and draw a slope.
  5. Release the mouse button.
