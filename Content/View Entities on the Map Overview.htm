View Entities on the Map Overview

When you select an entity in the Entity Hierarchy, it is displayed on the map as a black dot. Other entities are displayed as white dots (unless Data Visualization is enabled) To select multiple entities, press and hold the Ctrl key while clicking entities in the list.

When you click an entity on the map, it is highlighted in the Entity Hierarchy.

If entities or groups share the same latitude and longitude, an indicator appears beside the UWI to notify you that there is more than one well on that location. Use the Page Up and Page Down keys to scroll through the entities on that location.
