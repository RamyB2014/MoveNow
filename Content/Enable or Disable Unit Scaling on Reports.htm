Enable or Disable Unit Scaling on Reports

To disable unit scaling on reports

  1. Click the options icon () on the Reports tab or in the Print Preview.
  2. Select Force all auto-scaled values in reports to use standard units.

When the Force all auto-scaled values… option is not selected, the unit used is determined from the maximum value in the column(s). This means the units used could be different for each report.

When the Force all auto-scaled values… is selected, a standard unit (Mbbl, MMcf, bbl/d, Mcf/d, etc...) is used regardless of the maximum value in the column(s). This means the units used are consistent in all reports.

The Force all auto-scaled values… checkbox has no effect on values that are not auto-scaled.
