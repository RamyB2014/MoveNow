Scenario 3: Lessor and Overriding Royalty Exist; WI is less than 100%

Mr. Z owns the mineral rights in some land in Texas. He leases this land to Raven Resources, reserving a 1/8 royalty. (12.5%) Raven has a 100% WI. Raven Resources decides they need another investor so in exchange for an investment they offer Investor ING an Overriding Royalty of 1/5 (20%) of Raven Company’s Interest. Raven Company determines they still needs more investment, so they find a partner, JVA Exploration, and assign them a 25% WI.

Generally speaking, you should select the options below in this scenario:

  • Option A: Select this if you know your Lease Revenue Interest and the NRI.
  • Option B: Select this if you know the burden information.
  • Option C: Select this if you only have an NRI value.
