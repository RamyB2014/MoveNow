Enter Capital Spent before the Economic Calculation Start Date

If unrecovered capital remains prior to the Economic Calculation Start Date, do one of the following:

  • Enter the unrecovered capital amount as the Payout Reversion Value

    • OR

  • Use the Economics Detail—Before Tax Expanded report to determine when the capital will be recovered and use that date to enter a date reversion rather than a payout reversion.

If there are special contracts in place where a capital amount is used for a payout reversion but that amount is not entered in Economics | Prices & Costs | Capital Costs, enter the amount as the Payout Reversion Value.
