Edit Entity Information

To edit entity information, do one of the following

  1. In the Entity Explorer, right-click the entity you want to edit and click Edit Entity.
  2. Edit the information and click OK.

OR

  • Edit the information displayed in the Data View and click Save .

OR

  • Edit the information displayed on the Properties tab in the bottom left of the screen. Only bold fields are editable.
Changing the phase causes the forecast and volumetrics of the old phase to be overwritten with a new forecast.
