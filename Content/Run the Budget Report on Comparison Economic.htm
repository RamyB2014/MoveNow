Run the Budget Report on Comparison | Economic

The Budget Reports display values for the entities or folders, archives, plans, and reserves categories that are selected on Comparison | Economic, with detailed monthly and annual data and total and cumulative graphs.

When you set an entity as the baseline variance, the budget reports display the variances.

The following information is displayed for each of the comparison selections:

Selection Description
Before Tax Cash Flow Total, Cumulative, and Graphs of: Revenue, Royalties and Burdens, Operating Costs, Net Operating Income, BTCF.
Costs Totals of all Capital Cost types are displayed, plus data and graphs of Total Capital and Cumulative Capital.
Production Calendar rate and Total Production in BOE, plus Total Cumulative Production.

To display the Budget Report on Comparison | Economic, click Show Budget Report.
