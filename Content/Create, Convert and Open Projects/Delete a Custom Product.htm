Delete a Custom Product

You can only remove a custom product from your project if the product is not being used in a Product List.

To remove a custom product from a product list

  1. In the Tools menu, point to Global Project Data and click Products.
  2. Click the Product Lists tab.
  3. In the list on the left, click the product list that contains the product you want to remove.
  4. Move the product from the Selected column to the Available column.
  5. Repeat steps for any other product lists the product belongs to.
  6. Click OK.

To delete a custom product from your project

  1. In the Tools menu, point to Global Project Data and click Products.
  2. In the list on the left, click the product you want to remove.
  3. Click Remove and OK.
