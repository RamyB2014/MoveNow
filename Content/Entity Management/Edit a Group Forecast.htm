Edit a Group Forecast

When you create a group, automatically creates a manual forecast (a summation of the child wells’ forecasts). When a group has a manual summation forecast, decline parameters do not appear on Predictions | Declines and the columns on Predictions | Data are grey.

If you want a forecast that can be edited, you can best fit the group. However, you must manually remove the summation data from Predictions | Data.

After best fitting the group, you can return to the manual forecast by deleting the best fit forecast and clicking Recalculate on Predictions | Declines.

After best fitting the group, you can return to the manual forecast by clicking  on Predictions | Declines and then clicking Recalculate. See Recalculate a Group Forecast.
