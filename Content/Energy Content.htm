Energy Content

Energy content is calculated using the IMP (FPS) units from the GPSA Engineering Data Book.

Energy content is displayed in two locations. See the table below for a description of each.

Location Behaviour
Predictions | Gas Analysis, in the Heating Value column Based on the Master Gas Analysis.
Predictions | Data | Plant, in the Energy Content column Based on the Master Gas Analysis, but the value is recalculated when you enter a product ratio, rate, efficiency, or volume. You can override this value by editing the Default row or by entering a value in any month. The monthly value is applied to subsequent months, but you can override the value in any month.
