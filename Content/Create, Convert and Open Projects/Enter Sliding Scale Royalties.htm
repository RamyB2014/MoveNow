Enter Sliding Scale Royalties

All sliding scale overrides are entered on Economics | Interest & Royalties. There are three different places to enter sliding scale overrides on this tab, depending on who is receiving the overrides.

  • Royalties received
  • Royalties paid to partners
  • Royalties paid to mineral owners (appears only when Freehold is selected for Mineral Owner)

To enter a sliding scale override (payable or receivable)

  1. Select Economics | Interest & Royalties.
  2. In the window on the left, select the interest to which you want to add the sliding scale royalties.
  3. Beside the interests window, click the empty cell next to Sliding Scale Override and then click the ellipsis to open the Sliding Scale dialog box.*
  4. Edit the values and click OK.
If the Sliding Scale Override is not displayed in the list next to the interests window, click empty list at the top of the screen and select it.

To enter a Freehold Mineral Owner sliding scale override

  1. On Economics | Interest & Royalties, select Freehold as the Mineral Owner Type.
  2. Do one of the following:
    3. To Select And
    Use the crown calculations for the province based on the production category selected for the interest, Crown Equiv. for the Calculation Type Finish.
    Enter a percentage, Royalty % for the Calculation Type Enter a percentage in the Royalty field.
    Enter sliding scale royalty parameters, Royalty % for the Calculation Type Click the ellipse in the Royalty field and enter the sliding scale parameters.
