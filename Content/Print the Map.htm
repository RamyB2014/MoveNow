Print the Map

Once you have configured your map to display the desired entities and bubbles at the desired zoom level, you can print the map. When you print the map, all selected wells are printed, including those not visible on the Map tab.

To print a map

  1. On the Map tab, click .
  2. In The Print Preview click .
  3. In the Print dialog box, click OK to print an image of the map, along with an annotation page of all the wells displayed on the map.
You can also click the Export button in the Print Preview window to save the map in a different file format, such as a .pdf file.
