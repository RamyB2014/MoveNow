Project Options: SI/IMP Conversion Factors

Metric to Imperial

If enabled, the units are converted from Metric units to Imperial units. Liquid volume converts from m3 to bbl, and gas volume converts from m3 to scf.

Imperial to Metric

If enabled, the units are converted from Imperial units to Metric units. Liquid Volume converts from bbl to m3 and the Gas Volume converts from scf to m3.

Liquid Volume

The Liquid Volume field displays the factor that is used to convert liquids from one unit system to another.

Gas Volume

The Gas Volume field displays the factor used to convert gas from one unit system to another.
