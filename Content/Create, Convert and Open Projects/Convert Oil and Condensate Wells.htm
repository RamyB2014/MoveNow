Convert Oil and Condensate Wells

The Convert Oil/Condensate tool converts oil forecasts to condensate or condensate forecasts to oil.

To convert oil forecasts to condensate or condensate forecasts to oil

  1. Select the entity to be converted or filter to the entities to be converted.
  2. On the Administration menu, point to Data Cleanup and click Convert Oil/Condensate.
  3. Select Convert Oil to Cond. or Convert Cond. to Oil.
  4. If you want to convert the children of groups, select Apply to children of groups.
  5. Click OK.
