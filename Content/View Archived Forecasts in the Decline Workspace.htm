View Archived Forecasts in the Decline Workspace

On Predictions | Workspace, you can view archived forecasts and compare them to the active forecast (on Predictions | Declines) or other forecasts in the decline workspace. Archived forecasts are not editable.

To view archived forecasts in the Decline Workspace

  1. Go to Predictions | Workspace.
  2. Next to View at the top of the screen, select the Archive(s) you want to view. (If you want to compare archived forecasts to the Active forecast, also select Active.)
