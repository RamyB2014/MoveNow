Incremental Report Overview

There are four Incremental Reports:

  • Incremental Production
  • Incremental Production Variance
  • Incremental Capital
  • Incremental Capital Variance

The incremental production and capital reports identify reserves or capital spent in a specified year.

The incremental production variance reports identify annual incremental reserves and capital spending additions by comparing the current results (in the specified scenario) with past results (in the specified year).
