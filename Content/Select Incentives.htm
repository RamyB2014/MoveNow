Select Incentives

The incentives available for selection depend on the jurisdiction and phase of the entity. Once you select an incentive, additional parameters required for that incentive are displayed.

For dollar-based incentives, you can enter a Balance Effective Date, which enables you to specify when the Gross Balance Remaining should be decremented. If you do not enter a Balance Effective Date, the balance is decremented from the production start date.

See Enter a Payout Reversion or Incentive Balance and Effective Date.

To select incentives

  1. Go to Economics | Interests & Royalties.
  2. Under Incentives, select an Incentive Type.
  3. Enter the required incentive calculation parameters.
