Exclude a Well from a Type Well

Excluding a well from a type well only excludes its data from the type well calculation—it does not remove the well (see Remove Wells from a Type Well). When you exclude a well, you can easily include it again or choose to remove it entirely from the type well.

To exclude a well

  1. From the Entity Type list in the main toolbar, select Type Wells.
  2. Select the type well to edit.
  3. On the Type Well tab, click the curve of the well you want to exclude (or select the well in the Explorer).

Click Exclude and then click Recalculate.

To include the well again, click Include and then click Recalculate.
