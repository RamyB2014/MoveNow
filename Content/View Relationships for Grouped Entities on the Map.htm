View Relationships for Grouped Entities on the Map

The map displays entity relationships by drawing lines between parent and child entities. The Relationships checkbox in the map options is enabled by default. Use the procedure below to enable or disable the Relationships checkbox.

To view the relationships for grouped entities

  1. Select an entity (either on the map or in the Entity Hierarchy).
  2. On the Map tab, click  and then click .
  3. Select or deselect the Relationships checkbox.
  4. Click OK.

The relationship between the entities (i.e. all entities that make up a group) will be indicated by a line connecting all of the entities.
