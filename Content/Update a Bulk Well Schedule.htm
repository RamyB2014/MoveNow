Update a Bulk Well Schedule

If you edit the schedule’s source well or the schedule’s definitions on Bulk Well Generator| Definition, you need to update the schedule on Bulk Well Generator | Timetable.

On Bulk Well Generator | Timetable, three symbols are used to indicate what will be updated:

  • A red dot () indicates the source well was edited and its parameters will be updated.
  • A sunburst () indicates a well has been added on the Definition tab and updating the timetable will add a new well.
  • A red X () indicates a well has been removed on the Definition tab and updating the timetable will remove the well.

To update a well schedule

  1. Go to Bulk Well Generator | Timetable and click Generate Schedule.
    The Generate Bulk Well Generator dialog box displays the changes that will be made.
  2. Click OK.
