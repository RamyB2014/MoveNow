Convert Oil/Condensate

The Oil and Condensate Converter converts oil forecasts and history to condensate and vice versa. The converted entity’s product list is also changed from oil to condensate and vice versa.

To convert oil or condensate

  1. Select the entities with oil or condensate forecasts you want to convert.
  2. In the Administration menu, select Data Cleanup > Convert Oil/Condensate.
  3. In the Oil & Condensate Converter dialog box, select a conversion option.
  4. If required, select Apply to child entities.
  5. Click OK.
