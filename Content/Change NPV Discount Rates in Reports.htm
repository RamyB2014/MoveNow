Change NPV Discount Rates in Reports

Several economic reports and reserves reports include the Net Present Value at several discount rates or just one discount rate. The rates displayed in the reports are specified in the Project Options.

To change the discount rates displayed in reports

  1. From the Tools menu, select Options.
  2. In the Options dialog box, select the Project Options tab and click Economics on the left.
  3. Edit the Discount Rates and click OK.
Reports with only one discount rate use the middle value in the Project Options: Economics.
