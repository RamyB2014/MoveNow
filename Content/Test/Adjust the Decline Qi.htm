Adjust the Decline Qi

You can adjust the decline directly on the graph or in the decline parameters on Predictions | Declines.

To adjust the Qi on a graph

  1. On Predictions | Declines, right click the Rate/Cum graph, point to Gas Decline (or Oil Decline) and click Initial Rate.
    The mouse pointer turns into a hand.
  2. Use the hand to point to the Qi you want to use and left-click to set the Qi.

To adjust the Qi under Declines

  1. On Predictions | Declines click in the Qi field under Declines and enter the new Qi.
  2. Press Enter.
