Enter a Working Interest

Working interest is entered on Economics | Intersts & Royalties. You can enter

  • General working interest: If this is the only interest entered, it is applied to the capital and operating costs.
  • Operating costs: The interest percentage entered is applied to WI Operating Costs entered on the Operating Costs tab.
  • Capital costs: The interest percentage entered is applied to WI Capital Costs entered on the Capital Costs tab.
  • Facility Interest: The interest percentage entered is applied to FI Operating Costs and FI capital entered on the Operating Costs and Capital Costs tabs, respectively.
If the interest is not displayed, click the Interest Properties list and select it.
