Best Fit or Refit a Decline from a Point

Best fitting a decline from a point involves selecting a point in the production history. determines the best decline based on the production history from that point forward, ignoring the history before it.

To best fit or refit a decline from a point in production history you can click the mouse at any point in history while holding the Alt button or Ctrl button, respectively. The resulting decline type depends on which button you hold.

  • Alt + Click (best fit): determines whether the decline should be hyperbolic, harmonic or exponential.
  • Ctrl + Click (refit): maintains the current decline type.

To best fit or refit a decline from a point in production history

  1. On Predictions | Declines, right-click the Rate/Cum graph.
  2. Point to Oil Decline, and click Best Fit Oil Decline from a Point (or Refit Oil Decline from a Point).
    Your mouse pointer turns into a hand.
  3. Use your mouse pointer to click the point in history you want to use. best fits or refits the well based on the point you selected.

OR

  1. On Predictions | Declines, hold the Alt key (best fit) or the Ctrl key (refit).
  2. On the Rate/Cum graph, click the point in production history you want to use.
