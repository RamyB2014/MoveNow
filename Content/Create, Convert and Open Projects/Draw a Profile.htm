Draw a Profile

You can create a gas or oil profile by selecting points in the production history. Once you select the points, uses them to create a decline.

To draw a profile

  1. On Predictions | Declines, right click the Rate/Cum graph.
  2. Point to the decline type and select Draw Gas Profile (or Draw Oil Profile, etc.).
  3. Click on the points of production history you want to use in the decline.
  4. To remove a point, place your cursor near the point and press the Backspace key.
  5. Do one of the following:
    6. To Do this
    Cancel the profile, Press the Esc key.
    Finish drawing the profile, Press the Enter key.
