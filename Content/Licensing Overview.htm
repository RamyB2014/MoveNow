Licensing Overview

Each installation of requires license activation with a product key. When you start for the first time after installation, the License Manager is displayed and you can enter the product key. If you don’t know your product key, contact your administrator or contact .

Licenses are concurrent. When you use , you subtract one license from your company’s pool of available licenses. It is best to close when you are not using it in order to free a license for other users.

You must have an internet connection to use (unless you request a license to work offline) so can check the server for the status of the license. If your internet connection is temporarily lost, the license is not automatically revoked (to allow for an intermittent internet connection). However, if you are offline too long, you will lose your license.
