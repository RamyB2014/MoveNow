Use Manual Prices for an Entity

You can enter manual prices at the entity level in two ways.

calculates royalties based on royalty stream prices, not sales price for Canadian entities. Using manual prices may alter royalty calculations and should not generally be done.

Option 1

Override the prices displayed on Economics | Prices & Costs | Prices by typing over the prices. The values you enter manually are displayed with a grey background.

Option 2

Select Manual from the Price Set List on Economics | Prices & Costs | Prices and enter your own prices for each product.
