Nest Group Fields in Custom Table Reports

Group Fields can be nested within each other. This allows you to group your results by two or more fields.

To nest group fields

  1. Drag fields from the Fields and Formulas window and drop them into the Group window. The field on the top is the highest level of the grouping and fields below are lower levels of grouping.
  2. In the Group Fields window, right-click a group field, point to Expand Group and select Summarized or Expanded.

See Summarize Grouped Fields in Custom Table Reports.
