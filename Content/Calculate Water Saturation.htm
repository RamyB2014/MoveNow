Calculate Water Saturation

You can calculate water saturation while doing a volumetric analysis on Predictions | Volumetrics.

To calculate water saturation

  1. On Predictions | Volumetrics, click Calc. Sw.
  2. Select values for a, m, and n by selecting the appropriate lithology from the window on the right.
  3. Enter the connate water resistivity value in the Rw field (0<Rw>4).
  4. Enter the reservoir porosity (%) in the Porosity field (if it is not already populated from Predictions | Volumetrics).
  5. Enter the formation resistivity in the Rt field (0<Rt>10 000).
  6. Click Calculate Sw.
  7. If you want to use the calculated Sw in your volumetric analysis, click Use Calculated Sw.
    The Sw field on Predictions | Volumetrics is populated with the calculated value.
