Clear Flags

Only flags that are saved can be cleared with Clear All Flags . Entities flagged using the Flag Current Hierarchy Selection button () are saved automatically. Entities flagged on Wells and General Economics | Well Info and Custom Fields are not saved automatically.

To clear all flags: On the Standard toolbar, click Clear All Flags

To clear unsaved flags: Select the entities on Wells and General Economics | Well Info and Custom Fields, right-click, and select Clear Selected Flags.
