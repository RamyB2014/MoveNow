Designate Entities as In Reserves or Out Of Reserves

To designate entities as In Reserves or Out of Reserves

  1. In the In Reserves column of Wells and General Economics | Well Info and Custom Fields, select or deselect the checkbox.
  2. Click (save).

By default, filters the wells displayed in the explorer and in Wells and General Economics | Well Info and Custom Fields to in-reserves entities. When all entities are being displayed in the explorer, out-of-reserves wells are indicated with a yellow circle, as displayed below.

If a well is not “In Reserves”, it is not included in any jurisdiction calculations or reports even if it has not been removed from a jurisdiction. Also see Exclude Entities from Jurisdictions.
