Enter Burdens (Payable)

Burdens (Payable) are entered on Economics | Interests & Royalties. Burdens are explained below.

Burden Description
Gross Overriding Royalty (Payable) Royalty payable based on the gross proceeds from the production with no deduction for operating or capital costs.
Net Overriding Royalty (Payable) Royalty payable based on the net proceeds from the production.
Sliding Scale Royalty (Payable) The amount payable based upon a formula which is a function of production with restrictions applied.
Net Profits Interest (Payable) The amount payable based on a portion (%) of net profit from the property.
If the burden is not displayed, click the Interest Properties list and select it.
