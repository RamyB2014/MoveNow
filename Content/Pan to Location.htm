Pan to Location

Use the Pan to Location function to quickly go to a location using coordinates (e.g. N34 27.355 W108 17.5) or latitude and longitude.

To pan to a location

  1. On the Map tab, click  on the toolbar.

  2. Enter the location coordinates or the latitude and longitude.

  3. Adjust the Zoom Level, if required.

  4. Click Go.

The map will move to the location you specified.

You can change the latitude and longitude by clicking on the map.
