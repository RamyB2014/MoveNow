Enter Reversions

Reversions are entered on Economics | Interests & Royalties on a per interest basis. You can base a reversion on one of several Reversion Values, as explained below.

To enter a reversion

  1. In the Interests window, select an interest.
  2. To the right of the Interests window, click the empty cell next to Reversion Type and select Reversion Type (as described below).
    3. Reversion Type Description
    BOE Volume Enter the volume in BOE to be recovered before payout for the selected interest.
    BTCF Enter a value for Before Tax Cash Flow. The entity will revert when the cumulative result reaches this value.
    BTCF before NPI Enter a value for Before Tax Cash Flow less Net Profits Interest. The entity will revert when the cumulative result reaches this value.
    Capital (%) Enter the percentage of capital on which to revert. This will include all of the capital since the Economic Calculation date.
    Date Enter the date on which the BPO interest ends and the APO interest begins.
    Fiscal Regime The interest will revert based on parameters in the Fiscal Regime linked to the entity.
    Net Op Income Enter a value for Net Operating Income. The entity will revert when the cumulative result reaches this value.
    Net Rev before NPI less Sev Enter a value for Net Operating Income less Net Profits Interest and Severance
    Payout The entity will revert on payout of all the capital since the Economic Calculation Start Date unless you enter a Reversion Value and Effective Date (in which case the value is decremented from the Effective Date). See Enter a Payout Reversion or Incentive Balance and Effective Date.
    Time (months) Enter the duration of the BPO interest in months as of the first month of production.
    Volume Enter the volume (MMcf or Mbbl) to be recovered before payout for the selected interest.
  3. For all reversion types except Fiscal Regime, enter a Reversion Value.
  4. For Payout Reversions, enter the Reversion Effective Date, if required.
  5. Enter the APO interests.
If the reversion is not displayed, click the Interest Properties list and select it.
