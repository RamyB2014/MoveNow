Best Fit or Refit a Decline from a Selection

Best fitting a decline from a selection involves selecting a segment of production history. determines the best decline based on that selection, ignoring the history before or after it.

To best fit or refit a decline from a selection of history, you can use the shortcut keys described in the procedure below, or you can select the options from the context-sensitive menu. To use the menu selections, right-click the graph and point to Oil Decline or Gas Decline and select Best Fit from Selection or Refit from Selection.

To best fit or refit a selection

  1. On Predictions | Declines, press Ctrl + B to best fit or Ctrl + R to refit a selection of history (or use the menu selections).
  2. Place your mouse pointer and the beginning of the history you want to select and hold the left mouse button.
  3. Move your mouse pointer to the end of the history selection and release the mouse button.
